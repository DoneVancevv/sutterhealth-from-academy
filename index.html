Annual letter from our Board Chair and President & CEO

Ken McNeely

Chair, Sutter Health Board of Directors

Ken McNeely

Chair, Sutter Health Board of Directors

There’s never been a shortage of opportunities, challenges or competition in healthcare, but today, patients' options and expectations are changing at an increasingly rapid pace. Health systems must transform to meet their patients’ needs or else they’ll be left behind. Sutter Health is committed to leading in this new era of healthcare, and in 2023, we embarked on an exciting new chapter for our organization. One that will build upon our century-long legacy of caring for our communities and continue to make healthcare better and more accessible and equitable in California.

We started by establishing a clear aspiration and strategy for Sutter Health. Destination 2030 is our roadmap to the future. It outlines how we will become a leader in the way we serve our people, our physicians, our patients, our communities, and our organizational health.
